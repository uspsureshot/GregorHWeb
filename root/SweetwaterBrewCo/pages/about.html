About Sweetwater Brewing Co.

"The wise son brings joy to his father, but the wiser son brings beer."

Sweetwater Brewing Co. was started by two beer enthusiasts as a hobby. It has now grown into one of the most popular breweries in the county. We proudly specialize in locally grown, small-farm ingredients and our storefront features an eclectic menu of world-inspired cuisine.

It is our pleasure to continue to expose fans to beers they have never tried before by brewing a wide range of styles and experimenting with different ingredients.
