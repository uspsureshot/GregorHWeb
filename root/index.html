About Me

Hello. I'm Tanya Maseem. Welcome to my photography portfolio. I have been a photographer when i got my first Konica film camera as a teenager. Over the years my interest only increased. I love to capture shots in natural light and am enthralled with the colors of nature. Apart from my hobby, I am currently a professional photographer. I capture beautiful moments during events like weddings, birthday parties and bridal showers. You can see a sample of them in my gallery.

Equipment I use:

– Canon EOS 60d

– Sigma 50mm 2.8 Macro

– Sigma EX 10-20mm / 4-5.6

– Canon EF-S 18-200mm

If you would like to contact me to hire me for an upcoming event, or are interested in buying any of my nature pictures, or if you just want to say hi, please send me a message throught the contact page and have a nice time browsing through my collection.
