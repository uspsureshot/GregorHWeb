FreshPlate is a lightweight HTML5/CSS3 framework that gives you the power to create web pages quickly with solid, contemporary styling.
build

We built FreshPlate mobile-first to give you a tool that supports your mobile-first, responsive designs. A 12-column responsive grid with one-column mobile default can be applied with the addition of a few FreshPlate classes.

Quick styling of headers and font choices with classes unique to FreshPlate means you can get a beautiful page up fast. Our first plug-in, Add Instagram, is available here on this site. With it, you can add a gallery from your Instagram account by pasting in a few line of code.
